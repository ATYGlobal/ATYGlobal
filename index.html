Programa UNICO: €1B+ in NextGen EU Funds Drives Universal Fiber Coverage Across Spain

The UNICO Broadband Programme has deployed over €1B in NextGen EU grants since 2018, raising fiber coverage to 95.2% — versus the EU average of 64%. The third call allocates €150M to 380,000+ unconnected premises. The UNICO 5G axis adds €448M for rural backhaul, targeting 100% coverage by 2025 under the PRTR Recovery Plan.

Germany’s Glasfaser-Vollausbau Law: Mandatory In-Building Fiber Rights & New Market Tensions

The Federal Digital Ministry’s TKG amendment grants operators a statutory right to deploy fiber into multi-family buildings covering 23.5M apartments. Mandatory open-access wholesale applies where duplicate networks are uneconomical, with phased copper migration timelines and expected constitutional challenges from property-owner associations.

Project Gigabit: UK’s £5B+ Programme to Connect Hard-to-Reach Communities by 2032

BDUK has signed 30+ contracts targeting commercially unviable premises across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, targeting 99% gigabit-capable coverage by 2032 via FTTP. The Gigabit Voucher Scheme provides up to £4,500 per premises. Legacy ADSL faces a mandated phase-out by January 2027.
